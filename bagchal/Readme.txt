It is a game of goat and lion. Lion kills goat if it finds space after goat. Goat has to block lion from moving. 

You have goats in hand. Place them in such a way that the tigers do not eat them. Tiger eats goat by jumping over the goat. You loose when goats are eaten. You win when tigers are locked and can't move.

You can click the block to place and move the goats. You can also click the goat to move it. You have to click twice to confirm your move.
